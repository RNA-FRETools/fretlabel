The following jupyter notebooks illustrate the process of creating nucleotide-linker fragments.

  • Deoxythmidine fragment building outlines how to calculate an electrostatic surface potential (ESP), appropriate capping groups for RESP fitting and how to patch an AMBER force field with the new fragment parameters

  • Nucleic Acid coupling chemistries describes all currently implemented coupling chemistries for RNA and DNA labeling
